The currently featured ism is:

Pyrrhonism: Pyrrho was a painter and poet who accompanied Alexander the Great and apparently underwent a philosophical conversion in northern India, fueling speculation that he was influenced by early Buddhism. He believed that tranquility could be achieved only by steadfastly withholding judgment about ethical matters because they cannot be reliably decided as good or evil, true or false.
