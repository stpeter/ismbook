hylozoism

[From Greek hule: wood, material, matter, and from Greek zoe: life.]

  1. (metaphysics) The view that all matter is endowed with life (similar to animism, pantheism, or panpsychism).

  2. (metaphysics) The view that all life is a property of matter (similar to materialism).
