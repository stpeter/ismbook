Socraticism

[From the name of the Greek philosopher Socrates (c. 470-399 BCE).]

  1. (ethics) Socrates' doctrine of intellectualism.

  2. (method) Socrates' method of asking questions in order to arrive at universal definitions of concepts such as courage and justice (the so-called "Socratic method").
